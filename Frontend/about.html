Our Story

​TechMayank, founded in 2025 by Mitthu Kumar (Mayank), is a platform dedicated to simplifying complex tech concepts. It offers accessible insights, tutorials, and reviews on topics like web development, AI, cybersecurity, IoT, mobile development, and cloud computing.

TechMayank aims to bridge the gap between theory and real-world applications, empowering developers and tech enthusiasts alike.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission

To demystify technology and empower individuals with knowledge that enhances their digital literacy, enabling them to navigate the tech-driven world with confidence.

Vision

To build a global community where technology is not just understood but embraced, where innovation is celebrated, and where everyone has access to the tools and knowledge needed to thrive in the digital age.
