Welcome to TechMayank’s Privacy Policy. This document explains how we process your personal data, your rights under applicable laws such as the GDPR (if applicable), and how you can contact us regarding your data.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Data

We may collect personally identifiable information such as:

We automatically collect information about your device and usage, including your IP address, browser type, referring pages, time spent, and pages visited.

We employ industry-standard security practices to protect your personal data. However, no system is completely secure. We advise users to also take precautions on their end (e.g., avoiding sharing sensitive info via public Wi-Fi).
